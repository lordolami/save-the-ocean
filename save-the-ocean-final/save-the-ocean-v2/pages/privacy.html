Privacy
Policy

Last updated: February 2025. How Blue Earth Foundation handles your personal information.

This Privacy Policy describes how the Blue Earth Foundation ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our websites, services, and programs.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (name, email, payment details when donating), information collected automatically (IP address, browser type, pages visited, cookies), and information from third parties (donation processors, social media platforms).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process donations, send receipts and impact reports, communicate about programs and campaigns, improve our services, comply with legal obligations, and prevent fraud.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies

We use essential cookies to operate the website, analytics cookies (Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site, and preference cookies to remember your settings. You can disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings or our cookie preference tool.

4. Data Sharing

We share data with payment processors (Stripe), email platforms (Mailchimp), analytics providers, and legal authorities where required by law. All third parties are bound by data processing agreements.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data; object to or restrict processing; and withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@blueearth.org.

6. Data Retention

We retain donation records for 7 years as required by tax law. Email preferences are retained until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained for 26 months. You may request deletion of all other personal data at any time.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: privacy@blueearth.org
